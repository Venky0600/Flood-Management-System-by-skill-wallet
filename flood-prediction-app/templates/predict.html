Parameter Guide
🌧️
Annual Rainfall
Total precipitation recorded over the past 12 months. High annual totals saturate soil, reducing absorption capacity.
🌦️
Seasonal Rainfall
Rainfall during the current monsoon or rainy season. A key indicator of short-term flood potential.
☁️
Cloud Visibility
Visibility distance under cloud cover in km. Very low values indicate dense storm clouds and imminent heavy rainfall.
💧
Humidity
Relative humidity percentage. High humidity (>85%) combined with other factors strongly indicates flood conditions.
🌡️
Temperature
Ambient air temperature (°C). Combined with rainfall data it helps calibrate evaporation and runoff patterns.
🏞️
River Level
Current water level relative to the standard baseline (metres). Elevated river levels are the most direct flood indicator.
